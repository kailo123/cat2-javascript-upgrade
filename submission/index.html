About Abraval Fish Point

Abraval Fish Point is located in Machakos County and is committed to serving freshly prepared meals and farm-fresh vegetables. What started as a fish only shop has grown to become a full hotel where different meals are sold. Customers can pre-order food before arriving or request home delivery services.

We pride ourselves on good quality ingredients, excellent customer service, and ensuring every meal is prepared fresh for our customers.
